# 公式清单

## 问题一 - 基准运行分析

### 功率平衡方程
$$P_{\text{wind}}(t) + P_{\text{solar}}(t) + P_{\text{buy}}(t) = P_{\text{sell}}(t) + P_{\text{eq}} + P_{\text{load}}(t) \quad (1-1)$$

### 购售电决策
$$P_{\text{buy}}(t) = \max(-P_{\text{net}}(t), 0), \quad P_{\text{sell}}(t) = \max(P_{\text{net}}(t), 0) \quad (1-2)$$

### 绿电三指标
$$\eta_1 = \frac{E_{\text{load}} - E_{\text{sell}} - E_{\text{buy}}}{E_{\text{RE}}},\quad \eta_2 = \frac{E_{\text{RE}} - E_{\text{sell}}}{E_{\text{load}}},\quad \eta_3 = \frac{E_{\text{sell}}}{E_{\text{RE}}} \quad (1-3)$$

### 吨氨成本
$$C_{\text{ton}} = \frac{\sum_t P_{\text{buy}}(t) \cdot c_t^{\text{TOU}} \cdot 1000 + E_{\text{wind}} \cdot c_{\text{wind}} + E_{\text{solar}} \cdot c_{\text{solar}} + OM_{\text{el}} + OM_{\text{nh3}} + DEP - R_{\text{sell}}}{36} \quad (1-4)$$

## 问题二 - 离散制氨调节MILP

### 运行时长约束
$$\sum_{t=0}^{23} x_t = H \quad (2-1)$$

### 逐时功率平衡
$$P_{\text{wind}}(t) + P_{\text{solar}}(t) + \text{buy}_t = \text{sell}_t + x_t \cdot P_{\text{TOTAL}} + P_{\text{load}}(t) \quad (2-2)$$

### 购售电互斥（Big-M）
$$\text{buy}_t \leq M(1 - g_t), \quad \text{sell}_t \leq M \cdot g_t \quad (2-3)$$

### 绿电指标软约束（松弛变量）
$$s_1 \geq 0.60 \cdot E_{\text{RE}} - (E_{\text{load}} - E_{\text{sell}} - E_{\text{buy}}) \quad (2-4)$$
$$s_2 \geq 0.30 \cdot E_{\text{load}} - (E_{\text{RE}} - E_{\text{sell}}) \quad (2-5)$$
$$s_3 \geq E_{\text{sell}} - 0.20 \cdot E_{\text{RE}} \quad (2-6)$$

### 目标函数
$$\min \sum_t \left[\text{buy}_t \cdot c_t^{\text{TOU}} \cdot 1000 + x_t \cdot P_{\text{TOTAL}} \cdot 1000 \cdot \bar{c}_{\text{om}} - \text{sell}_t \cdot c_{\text{grid}}^{\text{feed}} \cdot 1000\right] + \lambda_1 s_1 + \lambda_2 s_2 + \lambda_3 s_3 \quad (2-7)$$

## 问题三 - 连续制氨调节MILP（拆分建模）

### 半连续约束（per-device, 10%下限）
$$P_{\text{ALK}}^{\min} \cdot z_{\text{alk}}[t] \leq p_{\text{alk}}[t] \leq P_{\text{ALK}}^{\text{sys}} \cdot z_{\text{alk}}[t] \quad (3-1)$$
$$P_{\text{PEM}}^{\min} \cdot z_{\text{pem}}[t] \leq p_{\text{pem}}[t] \leq P_{\text{PEM}}^{\text{sys}} \cdot z_{\text{pem}}[t] \quad (3-2)$$
$$P_{\text{NH3}}^{\min} \cdot z_{\text{nh3}}[t] \leq p_{\text{nh3}}[t] \leq P_{\text{NH3}}^{\text{sys}} \cdot \eta_{\text{NH3}}^{\max} \cdot z_{\text{nh3}}[t] \quad (3-3)$$

### 产量约束
$$\sum_{t=0}^{23} p_{\text{nh3}}[t] \times \rho_{\text{NH3}} \times 1000 = D_{\text{target}} \quad (3-4)$$

### 氢气逐时质能平衡（核心创新）
$$p_{\text{alk}}[t] \cdot \eta_{\text{ALK}}^{\text{H2}} + p_{\text{pem}}[t] \cdot \eta_{\text{PEM}}^{\text{H2}} \geq p_{\text{nh3}}[t] \times \gamma_{\text{H2}} \times \rho_{\text{NH3}} \times 1000 \quad (3-5)$$
化简：$$p_{\text{alk}}[t] \cdot 14 + p_{\text{pem}}[t] \cdot 16 \geq p_{\text{nh3}}[t] \cdot 400 \quad (3-6)$$

### NH₃爬坡硬约束
$$|p_{\text{nh3}}[t] - p_{\text{nh3}}[t-1]| \leq R_{\text{NH3}} \cdot P_{\text{NH3}}^{\text{sys}} = 0.20 \times 1.5 = 0.30 \text{ MW/h} \quad (3-7)$$

### 目标函数
$$\min J = \sum_{t} \text{buy}[t] \cdot c_t^{\text{TOU}} \cdot 1000 + \sum_{t} \sum_{d} p_d[t] \cdot 1000 \cdot c_d^{\text{OM}} - \sum_{t} \text{sell}[t] \cdot c_{\text{grid}}^{\text{feed}} \cdot 1000 + P_{\text{green}} \quad (3-8)$$

## 问题四 - 离网运行与储能配置

### SOC动态方程
$$\text{SOC}(t+1) = \text{SOC}(t) \times (1-\sigma) + \eta_c \cdot P_c(t) - P_d(t) / \eta_d \quad (4-1)$$

### 逐时功率平衡（离网）
$$P_{\text{wind}}(t) + P_{\text{solar}}(t) + P_d(t) = P_c(t) + p(t) + P_{\text{load}}(t) + \text{curt}(t) \quad (4-2)$$

## 绿电指标软惩罚模型

$$P_{\text{green}} = 1000 \cdot (\lambda_1 s_{\text{self}} + \lambda_2 s_{\text{green}} + \lambda_3 s_{\text{feed}})$$

其中 $\lambda_1 = 0.5$, $\lambda_2 = 0.3$, $\lambda_3 = 1.5$ 元/kWh。
